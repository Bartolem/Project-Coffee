Coffee Machine

The coffee machine has made brewing coffee simple. The interface is designed in such a way that anyone can use it without any problems. There are four types of coffee to choose from. The tanks, which contain the products needed to make coffee, allow you to brew at least several cups of coffee, depending on the type.

Custom Coffee

Every coffee lover, will choose the right type of coffee for himself. Currently, you can choose from espresso (pure coffee), latte (coffee with lots of milk), americano (coffee with lots of hot water) and cappuccino (coffee with milk and lots of milk foam). You can also create your own type of coffee, in the creator adapted for this purpose.

Ingredients

The most essential ingredient for brewing coffee, are the coffee beans. However, without the other products like water or milk, it would not be possible to create a proper drink. If any of the products is missing, you have the option of refilling it.
