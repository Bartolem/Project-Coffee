The coffee machine has made brewing coffee simple. The interface is designed in such a way that anyone can use it without any problems. There are four types of coffee to choose from. Every coffee lover, will choose the right type of coffee for himself. Currently, you can choose from espresso (pure coffee), lattee (coffee with lots of milk), americano (coffee with lots of water) and cappuccino (coffee with milk and lots of milk foam).

The most essential ingredient for brewing coffee, are the coffee beans. However, without the other products like water or milk, it would not be possible to create a proper drink. If any of the products is missing, you have the option of refilling it.
